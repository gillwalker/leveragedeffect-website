Leveraged Effect is all about helping you save time and use less energy to achieve your big goals — both personally and professionally. If it was easy, you'd already be doing it.

Leaders Fight for the Highest Possible Good in the Lives of Those They Lead.

I want to help you be a fully engaged leader — one who brings high support and high challenge to your team. The kind of leader who can identify the tendencies that undermine their influence and limit their ability to grow. That's the work we do together.

I've been coaching and mentoring for over 20 years, and I bring 25 years of experience from the technology industry as a trainer, sales leader, and senior executive. I've sat in the seat you're sitting in. I know what the pressure feels like, and I know what it takes to move through it.

I've also had the privilege of leading a number of nonprofit Boards and facilitating leadership development events across the United States. That breadth of context — across industries, organization types, and leadership levels — shapes every engagement I take on.

If you think I can help you or your business take it to the next level, I'd love to have a conversation.

Why I Do This
