What It Is

A Common Language That Actually Works

5 Voices for Teams gives everyone in your organization a shared framework for understanding how they lead, how they communicate, and how they contribute. It goes beyond a personality assessment — it's an operating system for your team.

We deliver this through a combination of in-person sessions, visual tools, and an online platform that reinforces key concepts and tracks engagement over time. Engagements typically run 3–12 months or more, working with teams of teams across your organization.

How It Works

The Engagement Process

01

Discovery Conversation

We start with a 30-minute conversation to understand your team's specific challenges, goals, and context. No generic solutions — everything is tailored to where you actually are.

02

Assessment & Baseline

Each participant completes an online assessment that establishes their 5 Voices profile. This gives us a data-driven baseline and creates the foundation for everything that follows.

03

In-Person or Zoom Sessions

We work with your teams through facilitated sessions — preferably in person, and fully capable over Zoom. Visual tools and real-world application make the content stick.

04

Ongoing Reinforcement

The online platform delivers content and tracks completion over the 3–12+ month engagement. Dynamic reporting keeps leadership informed on engagement and progress.
